About

About Me?

Welcome to my portfolio website! I am a versatile professional with a strong background in community management, moderation, and marketing collaboration. As a dedicated community manager, I specialize in leveraging the power of Telegram to foster engagement, drive growth, and establish thriving communities. With my extensive experience in the decentralized finance (DeFi) sector, I actively collaborate with new DeFi projects and assist them in their marketing efforts. As a trusted marketing collaborator, I offer discounted marketing fees to these projects, helping them effectively promote their products and services to a wider audience. Additionally, I excel at building strategic partnerships and nurturing relationships with influencers and callers within the industry. By leveraging these connections, I ensure that my clients' projects receive optimal exposure and recognition. My unique combination of skills as a community manager, moderator, and marketing collaborator allows me to deliver exceptional results. I am passionate about supporting innovative projects, fostering meaningful connections, and driving growth in the exciting world of DeFi.
